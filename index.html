Joan Tremblay MNLP, CHt

Joan is a recognized professional speaker, facilitator, Master Practitioner and certified Trainer of NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), a certified NLP Performance Coach, and a trained Mediator. Since 1995 Joan has given over 1600 seminars, trained over 30,000 employees and has written over 35 programs in the areas of Interpersonal Communication and Leadership. She has hosted 2 popular cable TV series and has trained in 48 of the 50 US states and all over the UK.

Joan specializes in interpersonal communication and presentation skills.

Edward McLoughlin PhD, MBA, MNLP

Ed holds a PhD in Psychology and an MBA in Organizational Development, he is also a certified NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming) trainer and coach. He has given over 400 seminars and consults with organizations at all levels of management. He has spent over 25 years at a senior VP level in Fortune 500 companies.

Over the last 25 years Ed has trained hundreds of managers in communication skills, conflict resolution, an management techniques. He has completed the Human Resources Competency Training given by The SCPMA Southern California Personnel Management Association.
