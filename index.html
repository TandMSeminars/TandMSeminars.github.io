Get inspired, get prepared, get promoted

Celebrating over 20 years

Tremblay & McLoughlin Seminars & Coaching has helped over 50,000 people to improve their skills, develop personally and professionally, and become better communicators and leaders.

Let us help you become your best.

  • Employee & Career Development

    We use accelerated learning so information is not only understood and retained, but is applied.

  • Leadership Development

    Our training is customized to produce results by using your real work-life examples.

  • Coaching & Conflict Resolution

    We provide one-on- one and group coaching, as well as mediation services.

Our knowledge, experience, and dedication to understanding the dynamics of public sector environments separates Tremblay & McLoughlin's approach from other standard training organizations. Our results are guaranteed.

We have presented over 2000 customized full and half day training programs for managers and staff in city governments, utilities, and select corporations.

Our Team

  • Joan Tremblay MNLP, CHt title Joan is a recognized professional speaker, facilitator, Master Practitioner and certified Trainer of NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), a certified NLP Performance Coach, and a trained Mediator. Since 1995 Joan has given over 1600 seminars, trained over 30,000 employees and has written over 35 programs in the areas of Interpersonal Communication and Leadership. She has hosted 2 popular cable TV series and has trained in 48 of the 50 US states and all over the UK.

    Joan specializes in interpersonal communication and presentation skills.
  • Edward McLoughlin PhD, MBA, MNLP title Ed holds a PhD in Psychology and an MBA in Organizational Development, he is also a certified NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming) trainer and coach. He has given over 400 seminars and consults with organizations at all levels of management. He has spent over 25 years at a senior VP level in Fortune 500 companies.

    Over the last 25 years Ed has trained hundreds of managers in communication skills, conflict resolution, an management techniques. He has completed the Human Resources Competency Training given by The SCPMA Southern California Personnel Management Association.
